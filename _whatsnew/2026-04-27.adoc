New enhancements in the Keystone dashboard in the NetApp Console:

=== Renamed Provisioned capacity label
The label *Provisioned capacity* is renamed to *Provisioned volume capacity*. The updated label is also reflected in the related CSV and Excel reports.
//NSEKEY-23492

=== Forecast toggle for accrued burst
You can now use the *Forecast* toggle in the *Accrued burst* view of the *Consumption trend* tab to project accrued burst usage for the next 120 days from the current date. To learn more, refer to link:./integrations/consumption-tab.html#view-forecast-data[View forecast data].
//NSEKEY-23493
